About

Learn about First Order Exploration.

With over 30 years of experience in the field, Dan is a world-renowned geologist specializing in the discovery of oil and gas resources. His expertise in state-of-the-art technology, combined with his extensive knowledge and experience, has allowed him to provide unparalleled consulting services to clients worldwide.

In addition to his work in the field, Dan is also dedicated to sharing his knowledge through educational materials and resources. He has published numerous research papers and articles in prestigious journals and often speaks at conferences and events around the world.

Throughout his career, Dan has received numerous awards and accolades for his work and contributions to the field of geology. His commitment to excellence and dedication to his clients has earned him a reputation as one of the leading experts in theindustry.

Dan holds a Ph.D. in Geology from a top-tier university and is an active member of several professional organizations. These affiliations enable him to stay up-to-date with the latest developments and breakthroughs in the field, ensuring that he can provide the best possible services to his clients.

Outside of his professional life, Dan is a dedicated family man and an avid outdoors enthusiast. He enjoys hiking, camping, and exploring the natural world, which has fueled his passion for geology since a young age. He also actively supports various environmental and conservation causes to help protect and preserve the planet for future generations.
